Explore Our Research Products

Dive into our comprehensive library of research products, meticulously crafted to support both students and professionals. Our innovative tools include:


Calculate PMP Tab

Access information about Probable Maximum Precipitation (PMP) with ease. Simply input the latitude and longitude, and our advanced algorithm will generate the PMP value along with other relevant parameters. This tool simplifies complex calculations, making it accessible to users at all levels.


Dashboard for Identification of Construction Waste

Access our specialized dashboard through the Research Work tab. This tool identifies construction waste locations, currently covering areas near IIT Tirupati campus and several other IITs. By pinpointing waste sites, we aim to support sustainable construction practices and waste management.


Interactive Stories: The Journey of a Student

Step into the world of our Life Stories Tab, where we bring you the interactive and inspiring story of a civil engineering student. Follow their journey from childhood dreams to current achievements, experiencing the challenges, triumphs, and growth that define the life of a future civil engineer. Our storytelling approach makes learning engaging and relatable, highlighting the human side of engineering.
